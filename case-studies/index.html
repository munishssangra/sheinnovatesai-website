Series A SaaS Company — 7 of 10 Target Queries in 8 Weeks

Challenge: Zero Perplexity citations despite strong Google rankings for target keywords. Buyers in the AI productivity category were researching on AI platforms and the brand was completely absent.

What We Did: Implemented Service and SoftwareApplication JSON-LD schema across all product pages, restructured 12 pages for direct question-answering format, established Wikidata entity, submitted to Bing Webmaster Tools, built cross-platform citations on Reddit and Product Hunt.

Results: 7 of 10 target Perplexity queries citing the brand within 8 weeks. ChatGPT citations appearing within 10 weeks. Inbound leads from buyers "who heard about us through AI research" began appearing in CRM within 6 weeks.

— Founder, Series A SaaS Company, AI Productivity Category, US

B2B Tech Platform — Enterprise Shortlisting Before Sales Contact

Challenge: Enterprise buyers at target accounts were completing AI-based vendor research before engaging the sales team. The platform was absent from Perplexity vendor landscape summaries, causing it to be excluded from initial shortlists.

What We Did: Organization schema implementation, FAQ content targeting enterprise comparison queries, G2 and Capterra profile optimization, cross-platform authority building in enterprise media, Bing indexing setup.

Results: Appearing in Perplexity vendor summaries for 4 of 5 primary enterprise query patterns within 7 weeks. Sales team reported buyers arriving on calls with prior AI-researched awareness of the platform.

— Head of Growth, B2B Tech Platform, UK

AI Product Startup — Product Named in ChatGPT Comparisons

Challenge: Competitors being recommended by name in ChatGPT and Perplexity for core product category queries. The startup had better positioning but zero AI presence.

What We Did: AI SEO audit identifying 5 missing signals, complete JSON-LD implementation, Wikidata entry for the company and founder, content restructuring for 8 priority pages, Bing Webmaster Tools setup and URL inspection requests.

Results: Product named by ChatGPT in target comparison queries within 9 weeks. Inbound growth from AI-referred users began within 8 weeks.

— Co-Founder, AI Product Startup, Canada
