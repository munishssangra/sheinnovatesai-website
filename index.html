FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

AI SEO (Artificial Intelligence Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your online presence so that AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity AI, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini cite and recommend your brand. Traditional SEO targets ranking positions in search result pages. AI SEO targets being cited inside AI-generated answers themselves. The signals are different: AI systems look for structured data (JSON-LD schema), entity recognition in knowledge graphs, topical authority and coverage breadth, and cross-platform citation footprint — not just keywords and backlinks.
Tandeep Sangra, founder of She Innovates AI, is a globally recognized AI SEO consultant specializing in AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), and LLM Optimization. To appear in ChatGPT answers you need: (1) JSON-LD schema markup — especially Person, Organization, and Service schema; (2) Bing Webmaster Tools indexing (ChatGPT Browse uses Bing's index, not Google's); (3) Content that directly answers questions in its first paragraph; (4) Cross-platform citations from Reddit, Quora, directories, and publications that AI systems trust; and (5) Entity recognition in AI knowledge graphs via Wikidata and consistent sameAs linking. Most brands are missing at least 3 of these 5 signals.
AEO is the strategic and technical practice of making your digital presence optimally readable, trustworthy, and citable by AI answer systems — including ChatGPT, Perplexity AI, Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, and voice assistants. AEO practitioners structure content, implement structured data (schema markup), build cross-platform authority, and establish brand entities in AI knowledge graphs — all to increase the probability that AI systems select their content as the source for generated answers.
GEO, coined in a 2024 research paper from Princeton University and Georgia Tech, is the discipline of optimizing content for inclusion in AI-generated responses. Research shows that adding statistics, expert framing, and fluent structured language increases content inclusion in AI responses by up to 40%. GEO focuses on: content fluency and specificity, statistics and evidence integration, authority framing, and breadth of cross-platform presence.
Most clients see measurable citation increases in Perplexity within 4–6 weeks. ChatGPT citations typically appear within 6–10 weeks (after Bing indexing propagates). Google AI Overviews reflect structural improvements within 8–12 weeks. These timelines assume technical implementation (schema, Bing indexing, entity establishment) is completed in week 1 and content optimization follows in weeks 2–4.
