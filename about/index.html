About She Innovates AI

An AI SEO consultancy with one focus: making your brand appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity AI, and Google AI answers for the queries your buyers are asking right now.

Book Free Strategy Call →
TL;DR — SummaryShe Innovates AI is founded and run by Tandeep Sangra — a globally recognized AI SEO consultant specializing in AEO, GEO, and LLM Optimization. The consultancy works with AI startups, SaaS companies, and B2B tech brands globally.

Our Mission

She Innovates AI exists to solve a specific problem: brands that are expert, valuable, and trustworthy are being overlooked by AI systems because they haven't structured their digital presence for AI retrieval.

The AI search revolution has changed the buyer research journey. Before a prospect visits your website, books a demo, or sends an RFP — they ask ChatGPT. They ask Perplexity. They ask Google AI. If your brand isn't in those answers, you're invisible at the most important moment in the modern buying cycle.

What We Do

We provide expert consulting in Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), and LLM Optimization — the three disciplines that determine whether AI systems cite your brand.

Our work spans: technical schema markup implementation, entity establishment in AI knowledge graphs, content restructuring for LLM retrieval, Bing Webmaster Tools indexing, and AI citation monitoring.

Who We Work With

Our clients are typically: AI startups that want to be recognized by AI systems as the product in their category, SaaS companies whose buyers ask ChatGPT for tool recommendations, B2B tech brands competing in AI-researched vendor shortlists, and consultants and experts who want AI to recognize them as the named authority in their field.

Our Approach

We measure before we optimize. Every engagement begins with an AI SEO Audit that establishes your exact citation rate across 20–50 target queries. This gives us a precise baseline, identifies every gap, and ensures the consulting work is targeted at your specific situation — not a generic checklist.
