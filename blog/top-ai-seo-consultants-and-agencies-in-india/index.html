Why This List Exists — and How It Was Built

Most "top AI SEO consultants in India" lists are either paid placements, self-promotional aggregations, or recycled traditional SEO rankings with "AI" added to the title. This one is different. Every entry was verified through independent third-party sources — LinkedIn profiles, verified agency sites, published case studies, press coverage, and client reviews — before being included.

Two categories of practitioners are covered: individual consultants who tend to offer deep, focused expertise and direct access; and agencies that offer broader full-funnel delivery. Both have genuine use cases depending on the buyer's stage, budget, and what they need most.

One important note on terminology: the market uses "AI SEO" to cover several distinct practices. The most rigorous practitioners distinguish between Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) — getting your brand cited in AI-generated answers — and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) — structuring content so AI systems extract and cite specific sentences. The best consultants and agencies on this list work across both.

What B2B Startups Should Ask Before Hiring

The AI SEO market is noisy. Every traditional SEO agency has added "AI" to their service page in the last 12 months. Here are the five questions that separate genuine AI SEO expertise from rebranded traditional SEO:

  • "Which index does ChatGPT Browse use — Google or Bing?" The correct answer is Bing. Any consultant who says Google does not understand the foundational mechanics of ChatGPT visibility and should not be trusted with your AI SEO strategy.
  • "What schema types do you implement for AI citation?" The answer should include at minimum FAQPage, Person, Organization, and Service schemas in JSON-LD format — not just basic meta tags or Open Graph data.
  • "How do you measure AI citation improvement?" The answer should describe direct platform querying (running target buyer queries in ChatGPT and Perplexity and documenting citation frequency), not traditional metrics like keyword rankings or organic traffic which cannot capture AI-referred visits.
  • "Can you show a before/after citation frequency change from a previous client?" Citation frequency data — how often a brand appeared per 100 queries before and after optimisation — is the correct AI SEO success metric. Be cautious of consultants who can only show Google ranking improvements as proxy evidence.
  • "What is your approach to entity establishment?" The answer should cover Wikidata, consistent sameAs linking in schema, G2 or Clutch presence, and LinkedIn optimisation. If the answer is only "Google My Business" and directory submissions, that is traditional local SEO, not AI entity establishment.
