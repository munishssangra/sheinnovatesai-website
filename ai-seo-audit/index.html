What's Included in the AI Visibility Audit

Phase 1: Citation Baseline (Day 1)

We query ChatGPT, Perplexity AI, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini with 20–50 of your highest-priority buyer queries. For each query we record: whether your brand is cited, which competitor is cited instead, the exact phrasing used, and the source cited. This gives you a precise citation rate baseline — your starting point for all future measurement.

Phase 2: Technical Analysis (Day 2)

A full technical analysis of your current schema markup (or lack thereof), Bing Webmaster Tools verification status, robots.txt AI crawler permissions, llms.txt presence, entity recognition in AI knowledge graphs, and content structure analysis against the AEO and GEO frameworks.

Phase 3: Competitor Citation Analysis (Day 2–3)

We identify every competitor that is appearing in your target queries and reverse-engineer exactly why AI systems cite them over you. This becomes the prioritized action list — fix your specific gaps before building new signals.

Phase 4: 90-Day Roadmap Delivery (Day 3)

A prioritized 90-day roadmap with specific actions organized by impact and effort. Week 1 quick wins (Bing indexing, schema fixes) through 90-day authority building milestones. Includes a 1-hour strategy walkthrough call with Tandeep Sangra.
